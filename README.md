Comprehensive Air Quality Measurements from Major Cities Worldwide


- Domain :  Environment / Weather / Climate analytics 

- Dataset:  Comprehensive Air Quality Measurements from Major Cities Worldwide(Kaggle)
- Why this Domain?
   Air pollution is a major public health issue worldwide.
   Governments and cities need data-driven policies to control pollution.
   This dataset combines pollution + weather, enabling deeper insights.
   Suitable for EDA, visualization, and predictive analysis.

- What problem can be solved using this data?
   Identification of highly polluted cities and periods with unsafe air quality levels.
   Analysis of air pollution trends over time to assess environmental changes.
   Understanding the impact of weather conditions on pollutant concentration.
   Detection of days with hazardous air quality to support public health warnings.
   Providing data-driven insights to help policymakers design effective pollution control strategies.
